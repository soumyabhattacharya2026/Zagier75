Modular Forms, Multiple Zeta Values and Polylogarithms

A Number Theory Conference Celebrating the 75th Birthday of Don Zagier

The conference is organized to celebrate the 75th birthday of Don Zagier, whose work has profoundly influenced number theory, modular forms, multiple zeta values, polylogarithms, arithmetic geometry, topology and mathematical physics.

The conference will bring together leading researchers from around the world to discuss recent developments inspired by Zagier's ideas and their continuing impact across mathematics.

Scientific Themes

  • Modular Forms
  • Multiple Zeta Values
  • Polylogarithms
  • Automorphic Forms
  • Arithmetic Geometry
  • Motives
  • Algebraic K-Theory
  • Computational Number Theory
  • Mathematical Physics
